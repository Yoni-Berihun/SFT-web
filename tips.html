Advice spotlight

50/30/20 remix

Try shrinking wants to 20%, freeing 10% for textbooks or lab fees. Revisit the ratio at the end of each semester.

  • Split transfers the day your stipend lands.
  • Use two bank accounts to separate needs vs. wants.
  • Invest the surplus into your emergency fund.

Campus hack

Community buying power

Pool recurring purchases (data plans, transport cards, groceries) with roommates. Rotate who pays and settle inside the Split tab.

Average save: 12% Time to set up: 8 min Best for: dorm mates

Next best action

Automate “micro investments”

Every time you log a luxury spend above ETB 300, match 10% into savings. Use the Expenses rules tab to remind yourself.

Financial hygiene

Weekly money retro

Every Sunday, review wins and leaks. Screenshot your dashboard, highlight one thing to repeat and one to drop.

  • Check for duplicate charges and subscriptions.
  • Move small windfalls into your high-yield goal.
  • Update your wishlist so spending stays intentional.
